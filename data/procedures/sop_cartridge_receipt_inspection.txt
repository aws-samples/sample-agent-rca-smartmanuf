STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
TITLE: Incoming Inspection of Sensor Cartridges for NEXUS Immunoassays
DOCUMENT ID: SOP-CART-001
REVISION: 4
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2022-09-01
REVIEW DATE: 2024-09-01

DEPARTMENT: Quality Control - Incoming Inspection
AUTHOR: A. Lawson (QC Supervisor)
APPROVED BY: J. Martin (Quality Manager)

1. PURPOSE
   This procedure defines the incoming inspection requirements for Sensor
   Cartridge units used in NEXUS immunoassay kit production. sensor cartridges
   are critical components coated with specific capture capture reagents that
   determine assay performance.

2. SCOPE
   Applies to all sensor cartridge lots received from approved suppliers for use in
   NEXUS product manufacturing. This includes but is not limited to:
   - Sensor Cartridges NEXUS CK MB (CART-CKMB-001)
   - Sensor Cartridges NEXUS B2M (CART-B2M-001)
   - Sensor Cartridges NEXUS Troponin (CART-TROP-001)

3. REFERENCES
   - SPEC-CART-CKMB: Sensor Cartridge Specification for NEXUS CK MB
   - SPEC-CART-B2M: Sensor Cartridge Specification for NEXUS B2M
   - SOP-QC-010: General Incoming Inspection Procedure
   - ISO 13485:2016 Section 7.4 (Purchasing)

4. DEFINITIONS
   CART: Sensor Cartridge - plastic cartridge coated with capture capture reagents
   COA: Certificate of Analysis
   AQL: Acceptable Quality Level

5. REQUIRED EQUIPMENT
   - UV-Vis Spectrophotometer (EQ-SPEC-001)
   - Analytical Balance (EQ-BAL-003), calibrated
   - Optical Microscope (EQ-MIC-001), 10x-40x magnification
   - Barcode Scanner (EQ-SCAN-005)

6. PROCEDURE

   6.1 Receipt and Documentation
       a) Verify shipment matches purchase order (PO reference, quantity)
       b) Check packaging integrity (no damage, temperature indicator OK)
       c) Record supplier COA data in Incoming Inspection Log
       d) Assign internal lot tracking number

   6.2 Visual Inspection (AQL = 0.65, Level II)
       a) Sample per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4 sampling plan
       b) Inspect for:
          - Physical defects (cracks, chips, deformation)
          - Coating uniformity (no bare spots, streaks, or bubbles)
          - Cap color verification (product-specific identification)
          - Labeling correctness

   6.3 Analytical Testing

       6.3.1 Capture reagent Density (Critical Parameter)
             Method: UV absorption at 280 nm after elution
             Sample size: 5 cartridges per lot

             ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA BY PRODUCT:
             +------------------+-----------+-----------+-----------+
             | Product          | Spec Min  | Target    | Spec Max  |
             +------------------+-----------+-----------+-----------+
             | NEXUS CK MB      | 2.0 mg/mL | 2.2 mg/mL | 2.5 mg/mL |
             | NEXUS B2M        | 1.5 mg/mL | 1.8 mg/mL | 2.0 mg/mL |
             | NEXUS Troponin   | 2.5 mg/mL | 2.8 mg/mL | 3.0 mg/mL |
             +------------------+-----------+-----------+-----------+

             NOTE: Results at extreme ends of the specification range
             (within 10% of spec limits) should be flagged for review by
             QC Supervisor. While technically passing, extreme values may
             impact downstream calibration performance.

       6.3.2 Binding Capacity
             Method: Functional binding assay with reference target analyte
             Sample size: 3 cartridges per lot
             Acceptance: >= 95% binding capacity relative to reference

       6.3.3 Sterility
             Method: Per pharmacopeial method (USP <71>)
             Acceptance: No growth after 14 days

       6.3.4 Endotoxin
             Method: LAL kinetic turbidimetric (USP <85>)
             Acceptance: < 0.5 EU/mL

   6.4 Disposition
       a) ACCEPT: All criteria met - release to production warehouse
       b) REJECT: Any critical parameter fails - quarantine and initiate NCR
       c) CONDITIONAL: Non-critical parameter borderline - QC Supervisor
          review required

7. RECORDS
   - Incoming Inspection Report (form QC-INR-001)
   - COA archival (electronic and paper)
   - Retention samples (minimum 50 cartridges per lot, stored at 2-8 deg C)
