1. AUDIT SCOPE
================================================================================

Annual supplier quality audit of Surface Coating Solutions, qualified supplier of
CART (Sensor Cartridge) cartridges for NEXUS immunoassay products. Audit
covers manufacturing process, quality management system, and specific review
of lot CART-2023-06-002 linked to NEXUS CK MB calibration issue.

================================================================================
2. AGENDA AND DISCUSSION
================================================================================

2.1 Quality Management System Review
    - ISO 13485:2016 certification current (expires 2025-03)
    - No major findings from last external audit (June 2023)
    - Minor finding: Document control - some work instructions outdated
    - STATUS: Satisfactory

2.2 Manufacturing Process Review
    - Coating process parameters reviewed
    - Capture reagent immobilization protocol unchanged since qualification
    - Process capability study: Cpk = 1.2 for capture reagent density (acceptable
      but not robust)
    - Coating uniformity: CV = 5.8% (spec: < 8%)
    - STATUS: Acceptable with observations

2.3 Lot CART-2023-06-002 Review (Specific Focus)
    J. Martin presented the NEXUS CK MB calibration issue:
    - Batches 1009900940 onward showed elevated primary signal (>550 SIU)
    - Root cause traced to CART lot CART-2023-06-002
    - Capture reagent density at 2.4 mg/mL (within spec 2.0-2.5 but high)

    Surface Coating Solutions response (Dr. E. Fowler):
    - Acknowledged lot CART-2023-06-002 was at upper end of specification
    - Explained variation due to new capture reagent source material batch
    - Capture reagent raw material lot (AB-2023-04) had higher specific activity
      than previous lot (AB-2023-01)
    - Internal specification for capture reagent density: 1.8-2.6 mg/mL
      (wider than MedTech Diagnostics incoming spec of 2.0-2.5 mg/mL)

    DISCUSSION:
    J. Martin requested Surface Coating Solutions tighten internal specification
    to align with MedTech Diagnostics downstream process sensitivity. Proposed internal
    target of 2.0-2.3 mg/mL with alert at 2.3 mg/mL.

    Dr. Fowler agreed to evaluate feasibility and provide response by
    2023-10-15.

    ACTION ITEM: Surface Coating Solutions to evaluate tighter internal
    specification for NEXUS CK MB sensor cartridges (Target: 2.0-2.3 mg/mL)
    Owner: Dr. E. Fowler | Due: 2023-10-15

2.4 Process Controls and Monitoring
    - Statistical process control charts reviewed
    - Moving range chart shows lot-to-lot variation in capture reagent density
    - No trend or special cause detected in process
    - Variation attributed to capture reagent raw material variability
    - STATUS: Acceptable

2.5 Corrective Actions from Previous Audit (2022)
    - SCAR-2022-015 (Endotoxin testing frequency): Closed, verified effective
    - SCAR-2022-016 (COA delivery timeliness): Closed, improved from 5 days
      to 2 days average delivery
    - STATUS: All closed

================================================================================
3. AUDIT FINDINGS
================================================================================

MAJOR FINDINGS: None

MINOR FINDINGS:
  F-2023-001: Internal capture reagent density specification (1.8-2.6 mg/mL) is
  wider than customer specification (2.0-2.5 mg/mL). This allows production
  of lots that technically pass internal QC but may cause downstream
  performance issues at customer.
  Classification: Minor Non-Conformity
  Required Action: Align internal specification with customer requirements

OBSERVATIONS:
  O-2023-001: Process capability (Cpk = 1.2) for capture reagent density is
  adequate but not robust. Recommend process improvement to achieve Cpk > 1.5.

  O-2023-002: Raw material (capture reagent) lot-to-lot variability is the primary
  driver of finished product (sensor cartridge) variability. Consider enhanced
  incoming inspection of capture reagent raw material or qualification of second
  source.
