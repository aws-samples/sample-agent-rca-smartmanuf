1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This annual quality review covers the NEXUS immunoassay product family
manufactured during calendar year 2023. The review encompasses production
performance, quality metrics, customer complaints, regulatory compliance,
and corrective/preventive actions for key NEXUS products.

Key highlights:
- Overall batch release rate: 94.2% (target: >= 95%) - below target due to
  NEXUS CK MB calibration issues in Q3
- Customer complaint rate: 0.12% (target: < 0.15%) - within target
- One significant quality event: NEXUS CK MB S1 OOS investigation
- All CAPA actions from 2022 verified effective

================================================================================
2. PRODUCT PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
================================================================================

2.1 NEXUS CK MB (Reference 30421)
    Total batches manufactured: 15
    Batches released: 10 (66.7%)
    Batches quarantined: 5 (33.3%)
    Batches rejected: 0

    SIGNIFICANT EVENT: Starting July 2023, multiple consecutive batches
    showed S1 calibration signal exceeding upper specification limit (550 RFV).
    Investigation identified root cause as SPR coating batch variation
    (elevated antibody density in lot SPR-2023-06-002). See NCR-2023-0100,
    NCR-2023-0101.

    CAPA: CAPA-2023-0058 (SPR coating variation) - corrective actions
    implemented, effectiveness verification in progress.

2.2 NEXUS B2M (Reference 30420)
    Total batches manufactured: 12
    Batches released: 12 (100%)
    Batches quarantined: 0
    Batches rejected: 0

    No significant quality events during 2023 review period. All batches
    met specification.

    NOTE: Assembly line changeover procedures were reviewed and updated in
    Q1 2024 following historical near-miss on NEXUS Troponin (INV-2024-0067).
    NEXUS B2M shares assembly line ASM-LINE-01 with multiple products.

2.3 NEXUS Troponin (Reference 30422)
    Total batches manufactured: 18
    Batches released: 17 (94.4%)
    Batches quarantined: 1 (5.6%)
    Batches rejected: 0

    One batch quarantined due to reagent strip fill volume borderline result.
    Retested and released after investigation confirmed within specification.

2.4 NEXUS D-Dimer (Reference 30425)
    Total batches manufactured: 20
    Batches released: 20 (100%)
    No significant quality events.

================================================================================
3. NON-CONFORMITY SUMMARY
================================================================================

Total NCRs opened in 2023: 8
Total NCRs closed in 2023: 6
NCRs carried over to 2024: 2 (NCR-2023-0100, NCR-2023-0101)

NCR Breakdown by Category:
  Materials: 3 (including 2 related to NEXUS CK MB SPR lot)
  Equipment: 2
  Process: 2
  Documentation: 1

Most Significant: NCR-2023-0100 and NCR-2023-0101 (NEXUS CK MB S1 OOS)
  Root cause: SPR coating lot SPR-2023-06-002 antibody density at upper end
  of specification (2.4 mg/mL) causing elevated S1 signal.

================================================================================
4. CHANGE CONTROL SUMMARY
================================================================================

Total change requests in 2023: 12
Change requests by level:
  MAJOR: 1 (new equipment qualification)
  MEDIUM: 3 (including CR-2023-0087 - SPR lot qualification)
  MINOR: 8 (procedure updates, form revisions)

Notable: CR-2023-0087 - Qualification of SPR coating batch SPR-2023-06-002.
This change is linked to the subsequent S1 OOS events. Post-event review
identified that the change risk analysis should have included enhanced
initial monitoring for the first batches using the new SPR lot.

================================================================================
5. SUPPLIER QUALITY
================================================================================

BioCoating Solutions (SPR cone supplier):
  Supplier rating: 92/100 (Qualified)
  On-time delivery: 98%
  Quality score: 88/100 (down from 95/100 in 2022)
  Audit conducted: September 2023 (see meeting minutes)

  Issue: SPR lot SPR-2023-06-002 linked to NEXUS CK MB calibration problem.
  Antibody density within specification but at upper extreme. Supplier
  agreed to implement tighter internal control limits.

================================================================================
6. CORRECTIVE AND PREVENTIVE ACTIONS
================================================================================

CAPAs from 2022 (effectiveness verification):
  CAPA-2022-0032 (Calibration station lamp monitoring): EFFECTIVE - No
  recurrence of S1 drift. Closed.

CAPAs opened in 2023:
  CAPA-2023-0058 (SPR coating variation): In progress - Corrective actions
  include tighter incoming inspection criteria and supplier process controls.

================================================================================
7. RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2024
================================================================================

1. Tighten incoming inspection specification for SPR cone antibody density
   (propose 2.0-2.3 mg/mL for CK MB, reducing current 2.0-2.5 range)
2. Implement enhanced monitoring for first 3 batches after any SPR lot change
3. Review assembly line changeover procedures for all shared equipment
4. Conduct focused supplier audit of BioCoating Solutions coating process
5. Evaluate automated line clearance verification systems for assembly lines
