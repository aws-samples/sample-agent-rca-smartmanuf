NON-CONFORMITY REPORT: NCR-2025-0050
================================================================================

PRODUCT: NEXUS B2M (Reference 30420)
LOT NUMBER: 1011174640
DATE OPENED: 2025-07-18
STATUS: Investigation - Recall Initiated

TITLE: Customer complaint - wrong cartridge sachets in NEXUS B2M kit

DESCRIPTION:
Customer complaint received from Regional Medical Center (Customer ID:
CUST-FR-0456). Laboratory technician reported that sensor cartridge sachets inside
NEXUS B2M kit (lot 1011174640) were labeled as NEXUS CK MB (Reference 30421).
Visual inspection confirmed the presence of NEXUS CK MB cartridges (CART-CKMB-001,
no cap color identifier) instead of expected NEXUS B2M cartridges (CART-B2M-001,
blue cap).

CUSTOMER DETAILS:
  Customer: Regional Medical Center
  Customer ID: CUST-FR-0456
  Complaint Date: 2025-07-17
  Complaint Reference: COMPL-2025-0892
  Contact: Dr. M. Bridges (Laboratory Director)

AFFECTED PRODUCT:
  Product: NEXUS B2M (Reference 30420)
  Lot Number: 1011174640
  Manufacturing Date: 2025-07-12
  Expiry Date: 2027-01-12
  Quantity Manufactured: 2,500 kits
  Quantity Distributed: 1,200 kits (48% of batch)
  Distribution Markets: United States, Canada, Mexico

SYMPTOM:
NEXUS CK MB sensor cartridges (Reference 30421, component CART-CKMB-001) found inside
NEXUS B2M kits. Wrong cartridges are coated with anti-CK MB capture reagents instead of
anti-Beta-2 microglobulin capture reagents. Cartridges are physically identical in shape
but have different capture reagent coating and different cap color (CK MB: no color
identifier; B2M: blue cap).

IMPACT ANALYSIS:
CRITICAL - Patient Safety Risk
- Wrong capture reagent coating will produce erroneous B2M test results
- Patients may receive incorrect diagnosis based on invalid results
- No analytical detection possible at customer site (instrument accepts
  wrong cartridges without error)
- Risk Classification: Class I (Serious - potential patient harm)

IMMEDIATE ACTION:
- Product recall initiated for lot 1011174640 (2025-07-18)
- Urgent field safety notice sent to all customers who received affected lot
- Remaining undistributed stock (1,300 kits) quarantined and segregated
- All NEXUS B2M lots manufactured on 2025-07-12 flagged for inspection
- Assembly line ASM-LINE-01 placed on hold pending investigation
- Regulatory notification submitted to ANSM (French health authority)

PRELIMINARY INVESTIGATION:
Assembly records for 2025-07-12 show that NEXUS CK MB (Reference 30421) was
produced on ASM-LINE-01 immediately before NEXUS B2M (Reference 30420).
Changeover log shows changeover time of 12 minutes (SOP-ASM-003 requires
minimum 30 minutes). Line clearance checklist was not completed.

Operator: OP-015 (J. Reynolds) - reported production schedule pressure due to
rush order for NEXUS B2M.

ROOT CAUSE: Under investigation - suspected incomplete line clearance during
product changeover on assembly line ASM-LINE-01
