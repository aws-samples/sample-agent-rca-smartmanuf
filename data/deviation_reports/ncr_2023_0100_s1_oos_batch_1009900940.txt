NON-CONFORMITY REPORT: NCR-2023-0100
================================================================================

PRODUCT: NEXUS CK MB (Reference 30421)
LOT NUMBER: 1009900940
DATE OPENED: 2023-07-10
STATUS: Investigation

TITLE: S1 signal out of specification during calibration verification

DESCRIPTION:
During routine calibration verification for lot 1009900940, S1 signal reading
of 572.5 RFV exceeded upper specification limit of 550 RFV. Retest performed
on 2023-07-10 confirmed OOS result (575.8 RFV).

SYMPTOM:
S1 signal too strong, exceeding upper specification limit. Error code B18
(Invalid calibration) generated during instrument calibration.

TEST RESULTS:
  Initial Test (2023-07-09):
    S1 Signal: 572.5 RFV (Spec: 450-550 RFV) - FAIL
    S2 Signal: 101.5 RFV (Spec: 80-120 RFV) - PASS
    Error Code: B18 (Invalid calibration - S1 out of range)
    Instrument: NEXUS-001 (EQ-CAL-001)
    Analyst: QC-003 (A. Laurent)

  Retest (2023-07-10):
    S1 Signal: 575.8 RFV (Spec: 450-550 RFV) - FAIL
    S2 Signal: 100.2 RFV (Spec: 80-120 RFV) - PASS
    Error Code: B18
    Instrument: NEXUS-001 (EQ-CAL-001)
    Analyst: QC-005 (M. Petit)

IMPACT ANALYSIS:
Product cannot be released for distribution. Calibration verification fails
with error code B18 on customer instruments. Affected lot placed in quarantine
pending investigation outcome. Estimated financial impact: EUR 45,000 (2,500
kits at production cost).

PROCESS STEP: Calibration

IMMEDIATE ACTION:
- Lot 1009900940 quarantined (2023-07-10)
- Production hold on NEXUS CK MB pending investigation
- Subsequent lots (1009900941, 1009900942) also show OOS - quarantined
- Customer notification not required (product not yet released)

PRELIMINARY INVESTIGATION:
Equipment verification: NEXUS Calibration Station 1 (EQ-CAL-001) last PM on
2023-06-01 (WO-2023-0150) - all checks passed. Equipment cleared as root
cause.

Material review: New SPR coating lot SPR-2023-06-002 introduced at this batch
per CR-2023-0087. Previous lot SPR-2023-05-001 showed normal S1 values.
Correlation between SPR lot change and S1 shift to be investigated.

ROOT CAUSE: Under investigation - suspected material variation in SPR coating
