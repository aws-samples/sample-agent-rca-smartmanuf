CHANGE REQUEST: CR-2025-0045
================================================================================

TITLE: Update to assembly line changeover SOP (SOP-ASM-003)

PRODUCT AFFECTED: All NEXUS products assembled on ASM-LINE-01

CHANGE TYPE: Process
CHANGE LEVEL: MINOR

DATES:
  Submission Date: 2025-08-01
  Approval Date: 2025-08-10
  Implementation Date: 2025-08-15
  Effective For: All production runs after 2025-08-15

DESCRIPTION:
Update assembly line changeover procedure SOP-ASM-003 from Revision 2 to
Revision 3 to incorporate additional safeguards against component mix-up
during product changeover on shared assembly lines.

JUSTIFICATION:
Following investigation of NCR-2025-0050 (NEXUS CK MB cones found in NEXUS
B2M kit, lot 1011174640), review of the changeover procedure identified
opportunities to strengthen line clearance verification. The current procedure
relies on operator self-verification; the updated procedure adds mandatory
independent verification by a second operator and extends minimum changeover
time.

CHANGES MADE:
1. Minimum changeover time increased from 30 minutes to 45 minutes
2. Added mandatory independent verification (second operator sign-off)
3. Added barcode scanning step for component verification at each station
4. Enhanced clearance checklist from 8 to 12 items
5. Added photographic evidence requirement for line clearance
6. Mandatory supervisor sign-off before production restart

RISK ANALYSIS:
Current changeover procedure (Rev 2) allows single-operator line clearance
verification. Investigation of NCR-2025-0050 showed that time pressure can
lead to abbreviated changeover and incomplete clearance checks. Updated
procedure mitigates this risk through dual verification and extended time.
